Black Pixel Wukong is a pixel game adapted from and inspired by the popular game Black Myth: Wukong. It is also the first pay-once blockchain game. We aim to change the entire course of GameFi.

 

Connect

Black Pixel Wukong is the first pay-once blockchain game, where only purchasers can participate. Unlike traditional pay-once games, participation is limited and rotates based on NFT ownership.

Econometric models

We use statistical methods to analyze economic data, which is known as Econometric models. In the game, both New Game Plus (NG+) and achievements increase the rate of token production.

New Game Plus

After completing the game, players can restart at a higher difficulty while retaining some progress. NG+ will increase the token production rate, which will be our innovative reward mechanism.

Achievements

Achievements earned by completing specific tasks or milestones in the game will increase token output rewards in the next playthrough.
